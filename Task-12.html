Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Fake emails or messages that steal your personal information.

Malware

Malicious software that damages or hacks your system.

Ransomware

Blocks access to your files unless a ransom is paid.

Top 5 Safety Tips

  • Use antivirus and firewall protection
